Talbina

Generated by AI

Description

Talbina is a porridge made from barley flour, formed by adding milk and honey to the dried barley powder. The name comes from the Arabic word laban meaning milk, because of its resemblance to milk, as it is soft and white.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk or water.
  • 3 tablespoons barely flour or barely flakes.
  • 1 tablespoon honey.
  • 4-5 dates chopped.

Steps

For barley flour

  1. In a pot add barley flour and roast it for 2-3 minutes, until the flour turns light brown.
  2. Add milk or water and few dates in it. Simmer slowly over low heat for 5 minutes until it thickens, whisking regularly.
  3. Transfer in serving bowl. Add remaining dates and honey on topping.

For barley flakes

Start from second step and also add barley flakes here. Remaining is same.
