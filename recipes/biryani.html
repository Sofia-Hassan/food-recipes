Green Chilli Biryani

Description

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating in South Asia, traditionally made with rice, meat (chicken, goat, lamb, beef) or seafood (prawns or fish), and spices.

Have the very simple and easy Biryani recipi with low spicies yet delicious taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg rice.
  • 1 kg chicken.
  • 1 cup oil.
  • 1 cup yogurt.
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced.
  • 1-inch piece ginger, chopped.
  • 6-7 garlic cloves, chopped.
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped.
  • 3 tablespoons green chilli.
  • Salt to taste.
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper.
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seeds(zeera).
  • 1 lemon.
  • Handful of mint.

Steps

  1. Soak rice in water after washing them thoroughly. Put them aside for half an hour.
  2. In a cooing pot add oil and onions. Fry on medium flame until they get brown.
  3. Now add garlic, ginger and tomatoes. Fry for 2 minutes.
  4. And chicken, yogurt, green chilli and salt. Cook on medium flame until oil separates. (20 minutes almost)
  5. Add five and half glass of water. When water starts boiling add rice, black pepper and cummin seed. Cook on medium flame.
  6. When water almost dries add mint and lemon juice. Cover with kitchen cloth & lid and steam cook on low flame for -10-15 minutes.
