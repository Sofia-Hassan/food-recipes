Creamy Tomato Soup

Creamy Tomato Soup w/ Crostini 053109 by vmiramontes is licensed under CC BY 2.0

Description

This creamy tomato soup is full of flavor. You probably have all the ingredients you need to make this easy 30-minute soup.

Ingredients

  • 1 (29 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed chicken broth, undiluted
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped onion
  • Â¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine tomatoes, chicken broth, butter, sugar, onion, and baking soda in a large stock pot.
  3. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat; reduce heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Stir in cream until heated through.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
